## Compliance Posture Components

### 1. Contract Clause Registry

For each active prime contract and subcontract:

- Contract / task order ID, CO, COR, PoP, value.
- Contract type, set-aside, vehicle.
- **All FAR / DFARS / agency-supplement clauses** incorporated (full text and by reference).
- Specifically tag presence of: 52.203-13 (ethics), 52.204-25 (Section 889), 52.219-9 (subcontracting plan), 52.219-14 (limits on subcontracting), 52.222-6 (DBA), 52.222-41–43 (SCA), 52.222-54 (E-Verify), 52.225-x (BAA/TAA/BABA), 52.227-14 (data rights), DFARS 252.204-7012 (CUI/safeguarding), DFARS 252.204-7019/7020/7021 (NIST 800-171 / CMMC), DFARS 252.242-7005/7006 (accounting / business systems).
- Flow-down checklist for subs.

### 2. Control Inventory by Domain

| Control | Authority | Evidence | Owner | Last verified | Status |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------|---------------|--------|

Cover at minimum:

- **Accounting system adequacy** (SF 1408 / DFARS 252.242-7006).
- **Timekeeping** policy + training acknowledgments + sample audit.
- **Indirect-cost segregation** of FAR 31.205 unallowables.
- **Provisional vs actual indirect rate** monitoring + FAR 42.704 adjustment process.
- **Public-voucher review** (SF 1034/1035) controls.
- **TINA / certified cost or pricing data** controls.
- **Subcontracting plan** (FAR 52.219-9) goals and tracking + ISR/SSR filings.
- **FAR 52.219-14 self-performance** monitoring by contract.
- **FAR 52.203-13 ethics**: code, training, hotline, mandatory-disclosure log.
- **NIST 800-171 controls** with SSP / POA&M / SPRS score.
- **CMMC posture** by contract requirement.
- **Section 889 reps** + supplier-screen process.
- **SAM registration** + annual FAR 52.204-8 reps and certs refresh.
- **E-Verify** enrollment + queries.
- **AAP / VETS-4212** filings.
- **EEO-1** filings.
- **SCA / Davis-Bacon WDs** posted at every site; conformance log.
- **EM 385-1-1 / OSHA 1926**: APP, AHA, SDS, training records, near-miss/incident log.
- **NEPA / permits** files for active construction sites.
- **CPARS** records by contract; remediation plans for any "Marginal" or below.
- **FAPIIS** record review.
- **Export-control screening** (ITAR/EAR).

### 3. Audit Calendar

| Audit / Review | Frequency | Next Date | Lead Time | Owner | Status |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------|--------|

Standard cadence:

- **DCAA Incurred Cost Audit** — driven by submitted ICS, risk-based scheduling.
- **DCMA Business System** reviews (accounting, purchasing/CPSR, estimating, etc.).
- **OFCCP** audits — scheduling letters.
- **DOL Wage & Hour** complaint-driven.
- **USACE / NAVFAC / DLA** field audits — program-specific.
- **CMMC assessment** — self-assessment annually; C3PAO every 3 years for L2 (when applicable).
- **IPA financial audit** — annual.
- **SBA 8(a) Annual Review** — annual.
- **Aleut Corporation internal audit** — per Aleut Corp schedule.
- **CPARS interim and final** — per contract.

### 4. Evidence Management

- Centralized repository (SharePoint / Egnyte / GovCloud) for compliance evidence.
- Retention per FAR 4.703 — generally 3 years after final payment; longer for certain records.
- Access controlled; CUI-marked items in a CUI-authorized environment only.
- Versioning and change history.

### 5. Gap Analysis and Remediation

For each gap:

- **Authority** that drives the requirement.
- **Current state** vs **required state**.
- **Risk** of non-remediation (mandatory disclosure? business-system disapproval? CMMC ineligibility? OFCCP finding?).
- **Remediation plan** with owner, milestones, completion date.
- **POA&M entry** where applicable (NIST 800-171).

### 6. Mandatory-Disclosure Log

Tracked separately and with restricted access. Each entry:

- Date of discovery; reporter; subject contract.
- Nature of credible evidence (FCA, criminal, significant overpayment).
- Outside-counsel coordination.
- Disclosure to OIG and CO (date, content).
- Status and outcomes.

### Audit-prep checklist

For a specific audit, generate the request list, evidence package outline, single-point-of-contact, and pre-audit self-assessment.

## Hard Rules

- **Never alter or backdate compliance evidence.**
- **CUI** items handled only in authorized environments.
- **Mandatory disclosure** — credible-evidence findings under FAR 52.203-13 go to GC immediately, never papered into a remediation tracker.
- **OFCCP / DOL** correspondence goes to HR + Legal in coordination — do not respond informally.
- **Set-aside compliance (FAR 52.219-14)** is monitored continuously — not just at award.
